About Me

Hello! My name is Kya Carrington and I live in the DC Metro Area. I'm currently a fourth year at the University of Virginia majoring in Computer Science. Academically, I enjoy Software Engineering and CyberSecurity. This past year, I took a Penetration Testing class where I gained experience with tools like nmap, msfconsole, BurpSuite, and many more! I also created fuzzers, password crackers, and ran full pen tests on various Virtual Machines. This was one of my first exposures to hands on CyberSecurity training, and I loved it!

In my spare time, I enjoy working out, watching basketball games, baking, painting, and spending time with friends.
